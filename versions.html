Versions Of Helios Mass Mailing

HMM has 2 main versions. Both are under development

One Click,Limited People Mailed

In this version , there is a limited number of users you can mail , But after entering all rec_emails (Receiver emails) , you'll be 1 click away from sending the mails, altogether.

Unlimited Set of users,3 second cooldown

As the name says , It has a 3 second cooldown . It means that the program will ask you every recipent's email individually and will mail them one by one , unlike the other version in which it mails all the recipients altogether.
