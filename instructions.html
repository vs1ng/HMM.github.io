Instructions Before Use

Here are some important instructions to follow when using HMM

1. Email Account

For Helios to work properly , you'll have to go to your google account settings and turn on "Less Secure Apps" access so that you can log in with no issues whatsover

2. Account Security

Keep in mind , that every time you use the service you will have to use a google mail account to send the emails.This means that the account that will be used to send the emails will have to have all Authentication Factors turned off , such as a 2FA. You will also have to turn on "access to unsafe applications" setting in your google account

3. Usage

Install the .zip folders , extract them , go to the folder and ignore all the other files and run the .exe application file .
